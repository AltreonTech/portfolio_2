## ðŸ” How the System Works (Detailed Flow)

The platform is designed with two roles: **Customer** and **Business Admin**.

### **1. Customer Flow**
- The user visits the website and sees available **services** (haircut, consultation, therapy, etc.).
- They select:
  - A service  
  - A staff member (optional)  
  - A preferred date  
  - A time slot based on real-time availability  
- They enter basic details (name, phone, email).
- The booking is instantly confirmed, and:
  - A **confirmation email/SMS** is sent  
  - The appointment appears in the **admin dashboard**

### **2. Admin Flow**
The business owner logs in to access:

- **Dashboard:** Total bookings, upcoming appointments, open slots  
- **Calendar View:** Month/week/day view with color-coded appointments  
- **Service Management:** Add/update prices, duration, categories  
- **Staff Management:** Assign service availability  
- **Time Slot Controls:** Block dates, set working hours  
- **Notifications:** Auto reminders for both staff and customers  

Admins can **edit, reschedule, cancel** or manually add appointments.
