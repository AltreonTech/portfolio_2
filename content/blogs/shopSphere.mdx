The platform consists of **three core modules**:

### **1. Customer/User Platform**
The user browses products with advanced filters and search, adds items to the cart, places an order through a secure payment gateway, and tracks order status in real time.

**Flow**
1. User creates an account / logs in
2. Explore product categories and details
3. Add items to cart / wishlist
4. Checkout using Razorpay/Stripe
5. Receive order confirmation email & tracking updates
6. Review order history and rate products

---

### **2. Seller Dashboard**
Vendors manage product listings and sales from a dedicated dashboard.

**Features**
- Add/Edit/Delete product listings
- Manage inventory & pricing
- Process incoming orders
- Track sales analytics and revenue

---

### **3. Admin Panel**
Admin has full control over the platform operations.

**Capabilities**
- User management
- Product & seller approval
- Order & refund management
- Coupons & discount control
- Dashboard analytics & reports
