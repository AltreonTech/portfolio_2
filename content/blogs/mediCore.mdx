The MediCore system is divided into **four main user portals**, each with dedicated features and restricted access controls.

---

### **1. Patient Portal**
Allows patients to digitally register, book appointments, and access medical records.

**Flow**
1. Patient creates an account or logs in using OTP/email
2. Views available doctors & schedules
3. Books online or in-person appointments
4. Checks prescriptions, diagnosis records & lab reports
5. Pays bills online and downloads invoices
6. Receives reminders & push/email notifications

---

### **2. Doctor Portal**
Doctors manage appointments, medical history, prescriptions and reports.

**Features**
- Dashboard of todayâ€™s schedules & patient queue
- One-click video consultation link (optional)
- Digital prescription writing
- Medical record access & updates
- Lab test requests and follow-ups
- Patient history chart timeline

---

### **3. Staff / Reception Portal**
Used for front-desk operations and resource management.

**Capabilities**
- Appointment calendar board
- Patient registration & ID generation
- Bed/ward allocation for IPD
- OPD token system & queue handling
- Report & document upload

---

### **4. Admin Panel**
Full system control panel for managing hospital resources and users.

**Includes**
- Doctors & staff records
- Role & permission control
- Revenue, occupancy & performance analytics
- Pharmacy & inventory management
- Lab test management
- Blood bank & ambulance services
